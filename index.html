I'm interested in software development and politics. #### **This repo** I've tried a lot of blogging sites and each of them is lacking in some way or another. A friend suggested I try making my own and hosting it here on GitHub. I wanted something like Google docs, but more easily shared and not mixed into my Google account, or something like Tumblr, but more easily indexed and navigated. And in general, I wanted more control over the presentation. If hosting this on GitHub ever doesn't work out anymore, like if I get in trouble with them somehow, then I'll probably move on to making my own website. #### **Other places I can be found** - [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/irispol.bsky.social) - [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/fGdV7x5dk2) (my server) - [Tumblr](https://forthoseinterested.tumblr.com) - [Substack](https://northofqueen.substack.com) (no special paid content, just another place I copy things to) I don't use Facebook, Instagram, or X-Twitter.
